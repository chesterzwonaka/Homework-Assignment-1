Moi Moi Nigerian dish!

Moi Moi is a cherished Nigerian dish made from steamed blended beans, typically black-eyed peas or brown beans. This savory pudding is flavored with red bell peppers, onions, scotch bonnet peppers, and seasonings like crayfish and vegetable oil. Optional additions such as boiled eggs, smoked fish, or corned beef enhance its richness and texture. Moi Moi is loved for its unique, flavorful profile and creamy, satisfying consistency. Nutritious and versatile, it can be enjoyed on its own or as a side dish with rice, garri, or pap. For many, Moi Moi also holds cultural significance, often served at family gatherings and celebrations, making it a comforting and nostalgic favorite
